Pancakes

Description

Soft, fluffy, and every bit will just melt in your mouth. These are the perfect way to start your day

Ingredients

  • Plain Flour
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Combine dry ingredients
  2. Whisk in wet ingredients
  3. Pour 1/4 cup of mixture in pan
  4. Flip when the underside is done
  5. Flip again and serve with maple syrup
