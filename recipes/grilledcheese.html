Grilled Cheese

Description

Oozing with various types of cheese, this toasted sandwich is out of this world!

You can experiment with different cheeses, different breads, and different extras like mayo or mustard.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Tasty Cheese
  • Gruyere Cheese
  • Any other cheese
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Butter one slice of bread, and spread mustard on another
  2. Layer your cheese on the bread - the more, the better
  3. Butter the outside of your bread and put in the frying pan
  4. Fry till golden and all the cheese has melted
