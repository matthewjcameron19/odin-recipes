Burger Recipe

Description

A burger is similar to a sandwich - it usually has a cooked protein in between the top and bottom of a toasted bun

Ingredients

  • Burger Bun
  • Beef Mince
  • Burger Sauce
  • Onion
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato

Directions

  1. Shape the meat into a patty and start cooking - smash the burger down so it is flat
  2. Meanwhile, slice the onion and cook with the meat until caramelised
  3. Slice the lettuce and tomato
  4. When the meat is almost done, toast the bun
  5. Assemble your burger - add sauce to the bun, then add lettuce, tomato, the burger, and top with the caramelised onions
